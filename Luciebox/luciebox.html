What is the Luciebox

I was inspired by seeing a baby hyper-enthusiastically playing with one of my multiTimers. Originally thought of as a weekend project to create a box with some lights, buzzers and buttons, a year later we have the fourth version of a contraption with 22 applications and custom made pcb's. Yes, there is a manual, but, it's equally fun to just press all the buttons and see what's happening!
