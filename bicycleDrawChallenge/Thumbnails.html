These are the results of the drawing challenge we did on the 10th and 11th of June 2017 at the Vancouver Mini Maker Faire in the PNE, Vancouver. If you don't know what this is all about, you should sit down, and give yourself one minute to draw a bicycle. Don't google it, just do it. NOW. Stop reading! Do it, NOW!

At one point, we fell victim to the bicycle drawing challenge, we loved it so much we really wanted to share it!
The Makerfaire was an excellent opportunity to take the people by surprise!The one minute drawing challenge was a great success.
We collected and scanned over 250 drawings.
We love the whole range, from perfect to questionable over futuristic to hilarious! And we believe you will too! Enjoy
If you want to search for your own drawing and you have your red ticket, look for the last three digits on the ticket, that's the number of your drawing.
Google bicycle drawing test if you would like to learn more about this well known test!.
